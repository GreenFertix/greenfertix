Our Technology

At GreenFertix, we use advanced technology to produce green urea, ensuring a sustainable and cost-effective solution for agriculture.

Green Hydrogen Production

We generate green hydrogen using electrolysis, powered by Ethiopia’s renewable hydropower. This process splits water into hydrogen (H₂) and oxygen (O₂), eliminating carbon emissions.

Nitrogen Separation (Cryogenic ASU)

We obtain high-purity nitrogen (N₂) using a Cryogenic Air Separation Unit (ASU). This method cools air to extremely low temperatures, allowing oxygen, nitrogen, and argon to be separated efficiently.

Ammonia Synthesis

Hydrogen and nitrogen are combined in a Haber-Bosch reactor to form green ammonia (NH₃). This process is optimized to minimize energy consumption.

CO₂ Capture

We capture carbon dioxide (CO₂) from industrial sources using Monoethanolamine (MEA) absorption, making it available for urea production.

Urea Production

Green ammonia reacts with captured CO₂ in a urea synthesis reactor, forming high-purity green urea (CH₄N₂O) for agricultural use.
