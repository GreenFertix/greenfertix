Our Products

Currently, GreenFertix is in the development phase, working towards commercializing innovative green hydrogen-based products that align with Ethiopia’s sustainable industrialization goals.

Green Urea

A sustainable alternative to conventional urea, our green urea is produced using renewable energy and green hydrogen, reducing carbon emissions while supporting agricultural productivity.

Medical-Grade Oxygen

Leveraging green hydrogen production processes, we plan to supply high-purity medical oxygen to hospitals and healthcare facilities, enhancing Ethiopia’s medical infrastructure.

Industrial Gases

We aim to produce and supply essential industrial gases such as oxygen, argon and nitrogen for various manufacturing applications, strengthening Ethiopia’s industrial sector with locally sourced, eco-friendly solutions.

Our Services

At GreenFertix, we are committed to accelerating the green hydrogen economy in Ethiopia by providing essential services that bridge the gap between innovation, policy, and investment.

Capacity Building in Green Hydrogen & Its Derivatives

We empower local communities, industries, and policymakers with the knowledge and skills needed to develop and utilize green hydrogen and its derivatives in Ethiopia. Through tailored training programs, workshops, and knowledge-sharing platforms, we aim to build a skilled workforce that can drive a sustainable energy transition.

Advisory & Consulting for Foreign Investors

Ethiopia presents vast untapped opportunities in the green hydrogen sector. GreenFertix provides strategic consulting services to foreign investors, offering in-depth market insights, regulatory guidance, and feasibility assessments to unlock investment potential in green hydrogen and its derivatives.

Stakeholder Briefings on Green Fertilizer Benefits

We actively engage with key stakeholders, including government agencies, financial institutions, and industry players, to highlight the economic, environmental, and agricultural benefits of green fertilizers.

Advocacy & Ecosystem Development

GreenFertix is at the forefront of Ethiopia’s green hydrogen transformation, working to establish a clear roadmap for industry growth. By collaborating with government stakeholders, policymakers, and financial institutions, we contribute to shaping the strategies, policies, and directives that will facilitate Ethiopia’s transition into a billion-dollar green hydrogen industry.
