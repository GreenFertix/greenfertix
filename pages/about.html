About GreenFertix

Our Journey

Our journey started with rigorous research—understanding how to harness Ethiopia’s abundant hydropower to produce green urea at a competitive price.

 

While traditional fertilizers contribute heavily to carbon emissions, we took a different path: designing a low-cost, carbon-neutral production process that could make Ethiopia self-sufficient in fertilizer production.

 

Today, GreenFertix is not just a concept—it’s a company actively working toward building Ethiopia’s first large-scale green urea production facility, ensuring farmers have access to affordable, locally produced fertilizers while reducing the country’s dependency on imports.

Our Mission

At GreenFertix, our mission is to transform the agricultural sector by making sustainable, high-quality fertilizers the new standard.

 

We are committed to: Scaling up sustainable fertilizer production across Africa to support food security and environmental sustainability.

 

We believe that by leveraging Ethiopia’s clean energy resources, we can pioneer a new era of climate-friendly agriculture—one that is both economically viable and environmentally responsible.

Our Team

GreenFertix is driven by a team of engineers, botanist and business experts who share a common goal: to build a fertilizer industry that benefits both farmers and the planet.

 

We are building GreenFertix with transparency, resilience, and collaboration, knowing that the challenges of transforming an industry require both technical excellence and a strong commitment to sustainability.
